About Spotify Unwrapped

Discover your music journey all year round, not only in December.

Our Mission

Spotify Unwrapped was created to give music lovers insights into their listening habits throughout the year.

We believe that understanding your music tastes can help you connect with others who share similar preferences.

We do not profit from Spotify, ads or in any way.

How It Works

We use the Spotify API to provide insights about your:

  • Top artists and tracks
  • Genre evolution
  • Listening patterns
  • Music compatibility with friends
  • Personalized recommendations

Privacy & Data

  • Only accesses what you approve
  • Never sells your data
  • Allows friend-sharing control
  • You can delete your data anytime
  • Secure login via Spotify OAuth
