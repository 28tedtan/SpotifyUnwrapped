Welcome to Spotify Unwrapped

Ever wondered what your true music taste says about you? Spotify Unwrapped is more than just a playlist summary — it’s a live, dynamic experience that captures every beat of your listening journey. Discover your top tracks, genres, and artists in real-time and see how your music vibe compares with friends.

Whether you're into K-Pop, EDM, chill lofi beats, or throwback hits, we track it all and show you trends you never noticed.
